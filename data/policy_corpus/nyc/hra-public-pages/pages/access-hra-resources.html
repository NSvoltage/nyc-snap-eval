SMS/MMS Text Messaging Terms of Service

By providing your personal or other wireless number to HRA, you are providing consent for DSS/HRA to send you informational pre-recorded calls and/or text messages from or on behalf of DSS/HRA at the number(s) provided. Your consent to receive calls or texts is not a condition for services or benefits. 

To block all text messages from this number, reply with the word "stop". Text back "unstop" to receive messages again. By replying “stop” you may continue to receive text messages from other DSS/HRA/DHS telephone numbers. Carrier standard message and data rates may apply.
