ABAWD Work Requirement
On November 1, 2025, HRA was required to implement the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) eligibility requirements for Able Bodied Adults Without Dependents (ABAWD). ABAWD identified individuals will be required to comply with the ABAWD requirements beginning March 1, 2026.
Learn more about ABAWD work requirements
.
SNAP Scams
EBT scams are common.
Follow these tips to prevent thieves from stealing your SNAP benefits.
Freeze your EBT card using ebtEDGE. Only unfreeze it before you buy something, then freeze it again.
Check terminals in stores for credit card skimmers
Change your PIN and check your balance often
Don’t give out your EBT information over the phone

Applicant rights
You have the right to ask for an application for SNAP benefits. This office must accept the application as long as the application is signed and has a readable name (and address if you have one).
You have the right to apply for SNAP benefits online at
nyc.gov/accesshra
, in person, by mail, or through an authorized representative. A face-to-face interview may be scheduled at your request.
If all members of your household are currently receiving Supplemental Security Income (SSI) or plan to apply for SSI, you may apply for SNAP Benefits at the Social Security Office instead of turning in your recertification form at the SNAP office.
