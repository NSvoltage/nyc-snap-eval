The purpose of this GIS is to inform social services districts (districts) about the release of a 
revised SNAP Source Book. The July 2025 revised Source Book can be found at 
https://otda.ny.gov/programs/snap/SNAPSB.pdf.    
The SNAP Source Book serves as New York State’s SNAP program’s policy manual. It 
summarizes SNAP policy guidance and includes citations for federal and state SNAP 
regulation, and departmental policy directives. It contains new sections for the Elderly Simplified 
Application Project (ESAP) and NYS Combined Application Project (NYSCAP), as well as 
policy updates to each section.   
Districts are urged to access the revised online SNAP Source Book and delete/discard any prior 
versions. Source Book sections are periodically updated, and as these updates are posted, a 
GIS will be issued detailing the included changes. Revision dates for each section will be 
printed at the bottom of each page.  
Districts should contact their SNAP liaison if they have any questions.
